How it works

Three steps, then it runs on its own.

1

Connect your cards

Link your cards read-only in a couple of taps. We can see transactions and posted credits — never move money.

2

Match promise to payout

Tally records every offer you activate, then watches your statements to confirm the credit it promised actually posts.

3

See what you're owed

When a credit is late or missing, you get the merchant, the amount, and the activation date — everything you need to claim it.
